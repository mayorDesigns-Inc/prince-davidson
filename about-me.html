Everything about me

Prince Davidson is an astute and ambidextrous Business Engineer and Problem Consultant, serving as Managing Partner and Group Chairperson at Brainpharm Group Int'l Ltd. In this capacity, he and his team have been instrumental in initiating, nurturing, and expanding numerous start-ups, medium-sized enterprises, and large corporations. Recognized as a High-Valued Asset (HVA), Mr. Davidson excels in multidisciplinary business and managerial spheres, with a proven track record in the successful development, execution, and management of complex business portfolios with global interests spanning diverse industries such as education and academia, leadership and governance, intelligence, technology, construction, military and security, oil and gas, entertainment, media and public relations.

Mr. Davidson's versatility is demonstrated by his work on projects for various clients, including public institutions or governments across three continents and growing, including corporate institutions such MTN, Vodafone, Access Bank, First Atlantic Bank and International Organisations, DPs and CSOs such as the EU, UN, World Bank, BirdLife International, Coorperazione Per Lo Sviluppo Dei Paesi Emergenti Onlus (COSPE), Ghana Wildlife Society, to mention a few.

Prince Davidson is a certified Project Manager with a Post Graduate Diploma in Business Management and an Advanced Diploma in Human Resource Management from ABE-UK. His extensive qualifications also include Advanced Executive Training in Corporate Leadership and Governance, Oil and Gas Investment Management, Financial Risk Management, Public Relations, Advertising, and ICT. In 2020, he completed an executive course on Entrepreneurship in Emerging Economies from Harvard Business School (HarvardX). Notably, he has attained the Certified Lean Six Sigma Black Belt and Certified as an Advanced AI and Prompt Engineer. Currently, he is pursuing an advanced Master's program in International Law at Université Catholique de Louvain (UCLouvain Belgium), focusing on Human Rights, International Trade and Investment Law.

Beyond his executive roles, Mr. Davidson co-founded Renaissance Conglomerate and Renaissance Professional College with two friends. These flagship companies have successfully transformed into Africa Technical University College, a tertiary institution dedicated to developing technical professional education and grooming Africa's budding technologists and leaders. Not forgetting his own executive training and educational institute called the Davidson College of Tomorrow (DCT). He also runs an executive business consultancy firm, where as a business solutionist, his work helps to aid several start-ups and established companies in finding direction and scaling to greater heights. Mr. Davidson's continuous pursuit of knowledge and dedication to staying at the forefront of industry trends reflect his commitment to contributing valuable insights and driving success in the fields he engages with.

Furthermore, Prince Davidson's leadership extends beyond the corporate sphere. He is also a benefit entrepreneur and social mogul. He serves as the President of the Organization of Nigerian Nationals in the Diaspora, a parliamentary approved Nigerian diaspora organization through partnership with the All Nigerian Nationals in the Diaspora (ANNID). Moreover, he holds the prestigious position of Global President of the Global Africa – ACP Chamber of Trade and Investment, championing international trade and investment opportunities for the African, Caribbean and Pacific Group of States and serves as the Trade and Investment Consul, Trade Commissioner, Commercial Attaché or investment promoter as the case may be, for several of these states. His combined expertise and relentless drive make him a formidable force in the business world, empowering organizations to achieve unparalleled success.
