The Brainfarm Group

Brainfarm Group International Limited is the flagship company of all Prince Davidson's for-profit ventures, the company through its several subsidiaries offer comprehensive business development, advisory, and ancillary services to SMEs, large corporations and multinationals, CSOs, governments, private and public individuals, and enterprises across West Africa and beyond.

Our dedicated team of Business Engineers is equipped to assist businesses at any stage they currently are, from idea conception to exit, and with any objectives they are pursuing, whether just starting out in the ideation stage or concerned with legalizing their business by obtaining registrations and licenses or focused on growth, mergers, acquisitions or diversification.

We are committed to ensuring success in any of these endeavors seamlessly. For over a decade, our NextGen creative and innovative business solutionists have been actively involved in supporting businesses, forging the "next" in various sectors, and fostering responsible and sustainable growth.

Our satisfied clientele spans diverse sizes and industries, including small and medium enterprises, manufacturing and distribution, healthcare and pharmaceuticals, real estate and construction, technology and startups, media and entertainment, oil and gas, security, accounting, audit and tax, legal, and more. And we have assisted them in BDS related areas such as operational excellence, compliance, corporate strategy, HR, leadership, risk management and the finest corporate training around.
